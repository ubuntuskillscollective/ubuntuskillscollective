Strategic Partnership Opportunity

Partner with YouthSkills SA: Empowering South Africa’s Youth, Fueling Economic Growth

South Africa faces a dual crisis—youth unemployment and a critical skills mismatch. At YouthSkills SA, we are addressing these challenges by creating a pipeline of job-ready talent in high-demand sectors such as renewable energy, tech, and trades.

Why We Are Doing This

South Africa’s youth unemployment is not just a statistic—it’s a systemic failure. Over 3.4 million young South Africans are neither employed nor in education, while industries like renewable energy and IT struggle to fill roles due to skills shortages.

What We Aim to Achieve

  • Train 1,000 youth annually in certifications aligned with SA’s Critical Skills List (e.g., solar installation, coding, AI basics).
  • Place 70% of graduates into jobs, internships, or gig projects within 6 months.
  • Reduce dependency on social grants by creating a skilled workforce that contributes R500M annually to the economy by 2027.

Why Partner with Us?

Your impact will be measurable:

  • B-BBEE Compliance: Earn points via Skills Development contributions (SETA-accredited programs).
  • ESG Alignment: Support UN SDG 8 (Decent Work) and SA’s National Development Plan.
  • Workforce Pipeline: Access pre-trained talent in high-demand fields like renewable energy and IT.

Economic & Social ROI

  • Skills Development = Economic Growth: Every R1 invested in skills training yields R5 in GDP growth (World Bank).
  • Breaking the Poverty Cycle: 75% of YouthSkills SA’s target demographic relies on social grants. Employment cuts this dependency, freeing state resources.
